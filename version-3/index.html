Adrenaline
Forest

Challenge yourself on the high ropes! Students get to spend 3 hours climbing and ziplining along a series of courses, each designed for a different skill level.

Te Rauparaha
Arena

Go for a swim, ride the hydroslide, or relax in the spas. Students will have exclusive use of the entire aquatic centre, so they can race, play, or hang out with their friends.

Camp
Wainui

Pitch a tent at Camp Wainui, where a range of fun activities are available! Build rafts, go kayaking, and hike the beautiful Orongorongo river valley. During the hike, students will get to build fires on the riverbed, and maybe even toast some marshmallows!

Rangituhi
Walk

Traverse the Rangituhi walkway! Navigate to the summit, then descend into the beautiful forest of Te Ngahere o Tawa. On the way, students will learn about all the native plants and birds around them.

Community
Service

Spend a chill day with your friends, helping out in the community. Whether volunteering for a charity or school, or helping an elderly resident with gardening, students always have a lot of fun making Tawa a better place!
